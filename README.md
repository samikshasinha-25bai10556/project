## Project
Through this project, I aimed to create a quick and informative resource that students can refer to in their daily digital interactions. It also emphasizes responsible online behavior, cybersecurity awareness, and the importance of maintaining a professional digital identity.

This project is a comprehensive Digital Literacy Portfolio developed as part of my role as a Student Digital Ambassador. It consists of five structured tasks, each focusing on a key aspect of digital awareness, skills, and responsible online behavior. The goal of this portfolio is to educate and guide students in effectively navigating the digital world.

Task 1 focuses on creating a Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic, which presents important concepts such as safe internet practices, digital tools, and professional online behavior in a visually engaging format.

Task 2 involves building a Student Digital Portfolio, where I established a professional online presence by creating and organizing profiles on relevant platforms. This helps in showcasing skills, achievements, and academic work to potential employers and institutions.

Task 3 explores Coding and Collaboration Platforms, introducing useful tools that support learning, practice, and teamwork. This task enhances awareness of platforms that students can use for skill development and group collaboration.

Task 4 focuses on drafting a Professional Email and Etiquette Guide. It highlights the importance of clear, respectful, and formal communication in academic and professional environments, along with practical examples.

Task 5 is a Cybercrime Awareness Case Study and Prevention Guide, where I analyzed a real or realistic cybercrime scenario and provided preventive measures to help students stay safe online.

Together, these tasks create a well-rounded portfolio that not only demonstrates my understanding of digital literacy but also serves as a useful resource for my peers. The project emphasizes practical skills, awareness, and responsible digital citizenship.
