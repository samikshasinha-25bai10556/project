For Task 1, I created a Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic using Canva, which is a user-friendly online design tool with various templates and visual elements.
I selected a clean infographic template and customized it to present important aspects of digital literacy in a simple and visually appealing way.
My design highlights key topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette. 
I used icons, short points, and color coordination to make the information easy to understand and engaging for students. 
The aim of the infographic is to help my peers quickly grasp essential digital skills required for academic and professional growth.
One interesting aspect of creating this infographic was learning how to present information in a concise and visually effective format instead of using long text.
However, I found it slightly challenging to choose the right layout and balance between text and visuals. 
Overall, this task helped me improve my creativity and digital communication skills.
